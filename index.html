Frequently Asked Questions

What is generative art?

Generative art is art created using algorithms and automated processes, resulting in unique pieces that can be endlessly varied. Each piece in our collection is algorithmically generated, ensuring distinctiveness.

How can I purchase an NFT?

NFTs from the ApeChain project can be purchased through our marketplace once they are live. Be sure to follow our announcements for details on minting dates and purchasing instructions.
